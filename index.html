By joining the Cosmic Invention Club, you become a part of our supportive environment where ideas can grow and evolve. Members have opportunities to participate in collaborative projects, informal workshops, brainstorming sessions, and friendly showcases of new inventions.CIC believes that innovation often begins with simple curiosity, and through teamwork and experimentation, even the smallest ideas can become something remarkable. We look forward to welcoming passionate inventors who are excited to imagine, build, and create together.
